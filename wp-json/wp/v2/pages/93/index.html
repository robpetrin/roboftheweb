About Rob<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Hey! I’m Rob, and I’ve been developing websites for over 25 years. I got my start in the early years of the world-wide web when I was a pre-teen, and have loved it ever since. I’ve also spent time as a writer and educator, which led me to open this website. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

A lot of people find web development intimidating, when there’s really no reason to.<\/strong> I love teaching people about the web, and demystifying it. It’s possible for anyone to start web development without much preparation, and without paying for any software or hosting.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Background<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

I’m originally from Rhode Island, and am a life-long New Englander. I currently reside just outside of Boston, working full-time as a front-end web developer. I live with my wife, our dog \ud83d\udc15, and our red-eared slider \ud83d\udc22.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When I’m not working on websites, I’m working on this site, a YouTube video (or five), or my courses and other educational materials. When I’m not doing that, I enjoy Boston sports, All Elite Wrestling, and working on my house.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Educational & Professional<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

I graduated with my bachelor’s degree in Writing & Rhetoric from the University of Rhode Island<\/a><\/mark><\/strong> in Kingston, RI in 2009. I wasn’t immediately sure what to do with myself after college, but I’ve used my degree in every professional position since attaining it. Writing is a valuable skill taught to too few people.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I graduated with my master’s degree in Secondary Education from Saint Joseph’s University<\/mark><\/a><\/strong> in Philadelphia, PA in 2012. I then shifted to teaching roles, primarily in Central Massachusetts. I taught both Social Studies and Special Education in a variety of public, charter, and parochial settings.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I formalized my self-taught web skills by completing Scrimba’s Front-End Career Path<\/mark><\/strong><\/a> in 2021. Since leaving education in 2017, I’ve worked primarily in marketing agency settings, serving as both a Project Manager and Lead Web Developer, working mostly with clients on WordPress.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I launched my own creative agency in 2022, Petrin Development Services<\/mark><\/a><\/strong>, to better market myself, and the skills I can provide to small business owners in my area.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
